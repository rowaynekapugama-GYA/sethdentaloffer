Your questions, answered

How much does Invisalign® cost?+

It depends on your individual needs. You will receive a personalised quote after your consultation and assessment, and can ask about payment options on the day.

How long does treatment take?+

Treatment usually takes 4 to 18 months depending on your individual case. Your dentist will discuss expected timeframes at your consult. Individual results vary.

Is the consult really free?+

Yes. Your 30-minute Invisalign® consult is free. A $50 deposit holds your appointment and is fully refundable, or redeemed against treatment if you proceed.

What’s included in the $2,000?+

Digital scan and smile assessment ($500), custom take-home whitening kit ($450), retainers after treatment ($550) and a Seth Dental practice discount ($500), applied when you proceed.

Am I suitable for Invisalign®?+

Suitability depends on your individual circumstances and is assessed by your dentist at your consultation. Take the 30-second check to get started.
